Auction Helper

Enter Data ()

  1. In Excel (or a google doc) specify the items being given in a column called "Names".
  2. Add an additional column for each participant. Include each participants bids as rows. Bids for each participant will be normalized to $100.
  3. Copy and paste the spreadsheet into the text box below:
      Here is an example input from a spreadsheet (copy and paste it into the textbox to see the output for the example): 
      Names	Adam	Melanee	Jade	Chloe
Lamps	10	900	90	2
Chairs	9	800	1	1
TV	8	700	1	1
Family Room Sofa	7	600	1	90
Blinds	6	500	1	1
Kitchen Table	5	400	1	1
Shower Curtain	4	300	1	1
Bar Stools	3	200	1	1
Cocktail Table	2	100	1	1
Side Table	1	1	1	1
  4. Click "Run Auction".
